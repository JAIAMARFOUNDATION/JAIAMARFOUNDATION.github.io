Our Story & Vision

Jai Amar Foundation (JAF) is a multi-sector initiative founded by Jai Amar Kumar, driven by a purpose to empower communities, fuel economic growth, and build a better India. Our mission is to transform lives by engaging in impactful work across education, health, infrastructure, finance, and social service.

We believe in creating real change—through entrepreneurship, opportunity creation, community development, and inclusive service.

Vision:

To be a catalyst of transformation by bridging the gap between development and delivery across sectors.

Our Divisions

JAF Education

Bringing quality education to every corner—from rural students to digital learners. JAF Education promotes literacy, skill-building, and empowerment through innovative teaching models.

JAF Civil / JCB Works

We build the future—one project at a time. JAF Civil handles government & private tenders involving roads, schools, housing, excavation, and more.

JAF Health

Focused on grassroots health awareness, rural camps, and community wellness through collaborations with medical professionals.

JAF Finance

Empowering individuals with financial literacy, small business funding support, and digital banking education.

JAF NGO Works

Our roots remain committed to society. Through social drives, youth campaigns, and assistance programs, we stand beside those who need a push forward.

JAF Agro

Working with farmers, promoting sustainable farming, agri-finance, modern equipment, and market linkages.
