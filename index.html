Our Story & Vision

Jai Amar Foundation (JAF) is a multi-sector initiative founded by Jai Amar Kumar, aimed at empowering communities, building infrastructure, and providing innovative solutions across various sectors including education, health, agriculture, and finance.

We envision a self-reliant and empowered India, bridging the development gap across rural and urban regions.

Our Divisions

JAF Education

Promoting accessible, modern, and inclusive education models to reach every corner of the nation.

JAF Civil / JCB Works

Partnering with government and private entities for infrastructure, tender work, and machinery deployment.

JAF Health

Organizing health camps, rural health drives, and awareness programs for community well-being.

JAF Finance

Financial literacy, micro-funding support, and digital banking education for financial empowerment.

JAF NGO Works

Uplifting underprivileged communities with focused programs, drives, and social support systems.

JAF Agro

Advancing agriculture with sustainable practices, agri-finance, and market linkages for farmers.
